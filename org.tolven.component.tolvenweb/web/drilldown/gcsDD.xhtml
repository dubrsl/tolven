Interpretation

Generally, comas are classified as:

  • Severe, with GCS < 9
  • Moderate, GCS 9 - 12
  • Minor, GCS > 12

The GCS has limited applicability to children below the age of 36 months. Consider the Pediatric Glasgow Coma Scale.
