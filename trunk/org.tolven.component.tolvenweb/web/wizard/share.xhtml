Use this form to share data with someone else. In general, this form is specific to the context in which it is selected. For example, if you have the medical record of a specific person open, then sharing will be about that person.

Further, the nature of the information to be share depends on the account you select to share (send) information. In fact, it is possible For example, if you "share" while looking at a specific encounter or appointment for a specific person, then the sharing will be in the context of that specific encounter or appointment. The steps are:

  1. Select the provider or other destination account.
  2. If the other account is not yet established
  3. Select the data you will be sharing.
  4. Review the transaction and submit.

If this is correct, click the Next button below to proceed. Otherwise, click Cancel to cancel this sharing transaction.
